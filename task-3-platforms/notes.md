## Task 3 Reflection: Exploring Coding & Collaboration Platforms

For Task 3, I explored HackerRank for coding practice and Google Workspace for cloud collaboration.On HackerRank, I completed
a beginner-level Python challenge. This allowed me to practice fundamental syntax, which directly supports my ongoing efforts to learn Python for my 
academic projects and computational physics simulations. For the collaboration segment, I utilized Google Forms to construct a 5-question 'Digital Literacy
Awareness Quiz'. The quiz featured both multiple-choice and short-answer questions, focusing on essential concepts like safe internet practices 
and email etiquette.

These platforms will significantly aid my academic progression in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. HackerRank offers a rigorous environment to
continuously test and refine my programming logic, which is crucial for technical problem-solving. Simultaneously, gaining proficiency in Google Forms 
and linked Google Sheets enhances my capacity for efficient data collection and teamwork. These cloud tools will be invaluable for 
coordinating group projects, organizing research data, and streamlining collaborative assignments over the next four years.

# Task 1: Create a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic 

As a Student Digital Ambassador, the objective of this task is to create a one-page visual resource that helps our batchmates understand what Digital Literacy means
and why it matters.

## 📌 Requirements
* **Design Tool:** Use Canva, Prezi, Miro, or any free presentation/design tool of your choice.
* **Topics Covered:** The infographic or visual slide must cover at least 3 of the following topics:
  * What digital literacy is 
  * Useful digital tools for students 
  * Safe internet practices 
  * Professional online presence
  * Email etiquette 

## 📂 Deliverables
* **Repository Upload:** The final design (exported image or screenshot) must be saved inside the `task-1-presentation/` folder in this repository.
* **Report Write-up:** The final project report must include a 150-200 word reflection describing: 
  * Which tool was used 
  * What the design shows 
  * One thing found difficult or interesting during the creation process

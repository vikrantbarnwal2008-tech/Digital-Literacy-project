# STAY SAFE ONLINE: PREVENTION CHECKLIST
## Student Digital Ambassador Resource - CSE0001

1. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Always activate 2FA on your email and social media to add an extra layer of security beyond just a password.
2. Use Strong, Unique Passwords: Avoid using easily guessable information like your birthdate. Use a mix of symbols, numbers, and letters for every account.
3. Verify UPI Payee Details: Before entering your UPI PIN, double-check the name of the recipient displayed on the screen to ensure it matches the intended person.
4. Never Share Your UPI PIN/OTP: Remember that a UPI PIN is only required to SEND money, not receive it. Never share your PIN with anyone claiming to be from a bank.
5. Identify Phishing Attempts: Be cautious of urgent emails or SMS messages containing suspicious links. Check the sender's email address for misspellings.
6. Secure Your Home Wi-Fi: Change the default admin password on your home router and ensure your connection is encrypted (WPA2/WPA3).
7. Keep Software Updated: Regularly update your laptop's operating system and mobile apps to ensure you have the latest security patches.
8. Be Skeptical of "Too Good to Be True" Offers: Avoid clicking on ads promising free gifts or high-paying part-time jobs, as these are common entry points for fraud.

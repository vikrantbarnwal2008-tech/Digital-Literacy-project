## SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS CHECKLIST  
## Student Digital Ambassador Program - CSE0001  


## DO'S:
1.  Think Before You Post: Consider the long-term impact of your content, as digital footprints are permanent.  
2.  Use Privacy Settings: Regularly review and update settings to control who can see your personal information.   
3.  Respect Others' Privacy: Ask for permission before tagging others or sharing their information.   
4.  Fact-Check Information: Verify the authenticity of news or links before sharing to avoid misinformation.   
5.  Maintain Professionalism: Keep interactions respectful, as employers or internship coordinators may view them.   


## DON'TS:
1.  Don't Share Sensitive Data: Avoid posting your home address, phone number, or financial details publicly.   
2.  Don't Engage with Trolls: Do not respond to cyberbullies; instead, block and report them to maintain a safe space.  
3.  Don't Post Inappropriate Content: Steer clear of sharing illegal activities, offensive language, or unprofessional behavior.   
4.  Don't Overshare Your Location: Avoid using real-time "check-ins" that reveal your exact physical location.   
5.  Don't Plagiarize Content: Never claim someone else’s work, art, or writing as your own without giving credit.
